The Celebration

Experience the magical fusion of tradition and art at the Spanish Day of the Dead. From November 1st to 2nd, the heart of Madrid transforms into a living altar, honoring the departed with vibrant cempasúchil flowers, glowing candles, and festive music.

Date: Nov 1 - Nov 2, 2026
Location: Plaza de Cibeles, Madrid, Spain
Organizer: Asociación de Tradiciones Hispanas

Event Timeline

Nov 1 | 18:00

Opening Ceremony

The lighting of the Great Altar at Plaza de Cibeles, followed by a formal welcoming speech and candlelit procession.

Nov 1 | 21:00

Night of the Souls

A mesmerizing performance of traditional music and dance, featuring the "Danza de los Diablos".

Nov 2 | 10:00

Artisanal Workshops

Learn to craft sugar skulls (calaveritas), paper flowers, and traditional "pan de muerto".

Nov 2 | 20:00

Closing Fiesta

A grand carnival parade through the streets with skeleton costumes, floats, and fireworks.
